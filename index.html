FAQ

How does Charitio verifies charity campaigns?
We have volunteers, who is assigned for different regions all over the world. Once we need to verify the campaign, somebody is assigned and contact with initiator to be sure this is not fraud.
What benefits will I got if I donate money?
The main benefit is the sense that you helped someone without making profits! However there are might be some gifts established by organizators and 100% tax deductability.
